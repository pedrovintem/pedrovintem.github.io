Dark Light

This is my learning log, a website where I’ll keep track of my progress while studying for my BA (Hons) Music at the Open College of the Arts.

The structure of the website is pretty simple and intuitive. Each course will be broken down into parts, with each part containing Coursework (Research, Analysis, and Projects), Assignments (Submissions and Reflection), and Listening Log (Music analysis). There will be a separate part of the Listening Log dedicated to all of the music I’ve listened to, as well as my own reactions to it.

So go ahead and click on the menu to start exploring. If you’d like to read my most recent contributions, check here:
